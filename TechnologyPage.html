    Our Technology

    For growers that need a more effective and eco-friendly approach to crop protection. Culture Bot is an intelligent UGV robot that protects your crops and eliminates weeds, thereby reducing labour and removing herbicides from the picture. Unlike weed pulling and hoeing, Culture Bot is fully automated, and unlike herbicides our method is 100% sustainable and eco-friendly. Culture Bot saves you time, increases productivity, and eliminates headaches.

    See Demo

    DETECTS + IDENTIFIES WEEDS

    Culture Bot, is able to detect and distinguish different kinds of weed species using advanced imaging technology and artificial intelligence (Ai).

    DESTROYS CROP WEEDS

    Culture Bot has an advanced directed evergy device that targets weeds with precision, and then eliminates them with ease.

    FULLY AUTOMATIC

    Culture Bot is completely automated. That means it navigates your crops, recharges, and operates autonomously, without the need for your supervision.

    AUTOMATIC CHARGING SYSTEM

    Culture Bot uses an autonomous charging station to shelter and recharge itself. The station can be solar, generator powered, or connected to a power-source.

    ANALYTICS + USER APPLICATION

    Culture Bot gives you control with an easy to user application. You can manual override the robots, track them in real-time, and get location based weed kill counts.

    DAY + NIGHT, RAIN OR SHINE

    Culture Bot can operate 24/7. It protects your crops and kills weeds even while you sleep. Unlike spraying or hoeing, it works in rainy or windy conditions.
